Controls:
Use the Arrow Keys to manuver your player around the map.
To manually change each level, press the 'U' button on the keyboard to advance to the next level and the 'D' button on the keyboard to go back to the previous level.
To open this window again press the 'M' button on the keyboard.
In order to gain points you must hit each enemy and be attacking from above them.
 If you hit them too low you will die and respawn however you only have three lives so when those lives run out you lose.
For every three points you score, your player regains a life.
